Rachel Zapata

actress, singer, student

Rachel is an actress and singer in NYC. She was born in Washington D.C. and grew up in Ashburn, Virginia. She has a BA in Theatre performance with a minor in Music from JMU.

Rachel is currently a student of the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in NYC and an intern at the New Group Theater. At JMU, she was able to work with influential artists such as Ricky J Martinez, Artistic Director of the New Theatre, as well as pursue internships with The Hampstead Theatre in London and The American Shakespeare Center. She was nominated for the Irene Ryan Scholarship for her performance as Hecuba in Trojan Women.
